Picking Up an Apple

Step 1: Find an Apple

In order to pick up an apple, you must first have an apple to pick up. If you do not have an apple, you can find one by finding an apple.

Step 2: Make Sure Your Hand is Empty

You cannot pick up an apple if there is already something else in your hand.

Step 3: Place Your Hand on the Table

Rest your hand on the table that the apple is on. Keep your palm facing the apple, with your thumb side facing up.

Step 4: Move Your Hand Sideways

Move your hand along the surface of the table towards the apple.

Step 5: Wrap Your Thumb Around

Move your thumb so that it goes around the apple in the opposite direction that it was before.

Step 6: Grip the Apple

Tighten all of your fingers around the apple.

Step 7: Lift the Apple

Raise your hand while keeping a firm grip on the apple. Congratulations!
