How To Pick Up an Apple

Have you ever had an apple? Have you ever wanted to pick up said apple, but couldn’t? Well, you’re not alone. Every day, hundreds of thousands of people are born without the ability to pick up an apple. In fact, studies have shown that one in every one American has had a time in their life where they couldn’t pick up an apple. This website gives comprehensive guides on how to both pick up and put down an apple. That way, anyone who wants to move an apple from one place to another can do so with ease.
